# Improvement Brief — {{asset_type}}

> A structured creative brief for improving an asset that scored REVISE. Direction, not decoration. Every section should constrain the next attempt.

---

## Objective

_What the improved asset must achieve, in one sentence. The outcome, not the task._

## Creative direction

_The overall concept and feel to push toward. What the stronger version expresses, and the single idea it should commit to._

## Typography direction

_Specific type direction: weight, classification, tone, tracking, hierarchy. Name what to move away from (e.g. "default geometric sans") and toward (e.g. "heavy grotesque with tight tracking")._

## Composition direction

_Layout, focal point, balance, whitespace, scale, placement. What the eye should hit first, second, third._

## Color direction

_Palette intent, contrast targets, how color carries hierarchy and brand. Reference the brand palette and any contrast/medium constraints._

## Production constraints

_Hard limits the new version must respect: print process, color count, garment, viewport, aspect ratio, safe zones, minimum legible sizes, scalability._

## What to avoid

- _Specific clichés, tells, and prior mistakes the new version must not repeat._

## Final quality bar

_What "good enough to ship" looks like for this specific asset and brand. The line the revision has to clear to earn a PASS._
